I am an independent developer committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe gameplay experience. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how your data is protected while playing Hollow Apes.

Hollow Apes uses third-party services to enable gameplay features:

Photon VR (for multiplayer):
Photon may collect:

IP address

Region

Session data

Device information (model, OS version, platform)

PlayFab (by Microsoft, for player data):
PlayFab may collect:

The data collected is used only to provide multiplayer features, save your game progress, and improve the game experience.

What I Do NOT Collect:

I do not collect your real name, email address, home address, or personal contact information.

I do not access your voice, microphone, camera, or exact location.

I do not serve ads or use advertising or tracking services.

Game data is stored as long as your account remains active. You can request deletion of your game data at any time by contacting me.

Hollow Apes is not intended for children under 13 years of age. I do not knowingly collect personal data from children under this age without appropriate consent.
